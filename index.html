Daily News Briefer

A personal service run by Koby Beard.

Daily News Briefer sends its owner a once-daily news briefing by SMS and email, tailored to their saved topic preferences.

SMS consent

The user signs up for their own briefing and provides their own mobile number, consenting to receive a daily SMS summary of that briefing.

  • Message frequency: about 1 message per day.
  • Reply STOP to unsubscribe at any time; reply HELP for help.
  • Message and data rates may apply.
